About me

I'm a Computer Science student based in Portugal. I've been programming for several years and working with Unity for the last three, building 2D games, tools, and frameworks purely as a hobby in my spare time.

My work tends to focus on 2D physics, procedural animation, and gameplay systems — the kinds of things where tweaking a few variables can change how a whole game feels. Everything on this page is solo, personal work.
